Cold Room Installation and Repair Services

At MAXY Refrigeration and Appliance Services, we specialize in the installation and repair of cold rooms for both commercial and residential clients. Our team of experienced technicians is dedicated to providing top-notch service to ensure your cold room operates efficiently and effectively.


Whether you need a new cold room installed or require repairs on an existing unit, we have the expertise to handle it all. We use high-quality materials and the latest technology to ensure your cold room meets your specific needs and requirements.


Contact us today to learn more about our cold room services and how we can help you maintain the optimal temperature for your perishable goods.

Cold Room Components We Service

Insulation Panels

We install and repair high-quality insulation panels to ensure optimal temperature control in your cold room.

Door Seals

Our technicians can replace worn or damaged door seals to maintain the integrity of your cold room.

Refrigeration Units

We service and repair refrigeration units to ensure your cold room stays at the desired temperature.

Temperature Controls

We install and maintain advanced temperature control systems for precise monitoring of your cold room.

Compressor

Our team can repair or replace compressors to ensure efficient cooling performance.

Condensor Unit

We service condensor units to maintain optimal heat exchange and cooling efficiency.

Evaporator Coil

Our technicians can clean, repair, or replace evaporator coils to ensure effective cooling.

Sensors and Controls

We ensure that all sensors and control systems are functioning. Thermostats, alarms and lighting systems are all inspected and serviced by our team to ensure optimal performance.
