Fridge Regassing

Is your fridge not cooling as it should? It might be time for a regassing. Our technicians are skilled in safely and effectively regassing fridges to restore optimal cooling performance. Contact us today to schedule a regassing service and keep your fridge running smoothly.

Compressor Replacement

A faulty compressor can lead to inadequate cooling and increased energy consumption. If your fridge's compressor is failing, our technicians can provide expert replacement services. We use high- quality parts to ensure your fridge operates efficiently after the repair. Reach out to us for reliable compressor replacement services.

Fridge Relays

If your fridge is struggling to start or is making clicking noises, it could be due to a faulty relay. Our technicians are experienced in diagnosing and replacing relays to ensure your fridge starts smoothly and operates efficiently. Contact MAXY Refrigeration and Appliance Services for expert relay replacement services.
